# Job Identity

- `job_id` la identity bat bien, dang `job_000001`.
- `display_name` la ten hien thi, Unicode, khong dung lam khoa.
- `job_type` xac dinh worker/handler.
- `scope` co the la application, workspace, project, voice, style_profile, reference_asset hoac dataset.
- Owner IDs duoc luu bang stable ID: project_id, voice_id, style_profile_id, dataset_id va reference_asset_ids.
